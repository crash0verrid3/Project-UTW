Project UTW - A free, simple, fast, secure, and portable browser for your needs

Project UTW is a lightweight web browser created in Java..

Open-source

Project UTW is open-source. You can change it to your liking and do whatever you want with it. All you need is to give me credit for the original version and to make your version use the same license.

Have an idea? Let us know!

If you want a feature added, just let us know! We'd be glad to implement it for you and make it that much better. If you would rather make your own version, just make it - The source code isn't online for nothing!

Want to sell this? Let us know!

You can email us at thesuperuser2.0@outlook.com about wanting to sell a version or take full credit for a change. We'll be glad to work out a way where you can make money from the project. Even if you don't want to contact us, you can take credit for a change. Just make sure to read the README.txt included in the repository and JAR file.
